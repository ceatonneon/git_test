Some of my favorite sites

Wikipedia

One of my favorite websites is called wikipedia. This terrific site allows ordinary users to enter encyclopedia definitions. Over time, the entries can be as reliable as a commercial encyclopedia, and a lot more complete.

Dummies

You can find out a lot about upcoming and current Dummies books at www.dummies.com. You might even find this book mentioned there.

PopURLS

If you want to know what's happening on the Internet today, check out popurls.com. This site aggregates a bunch of social networking sites.
